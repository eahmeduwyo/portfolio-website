About Estiak Ahmed

Estiak Ahmed is a passionate Graduate Teaching Assistant and Master’s candidate in Architectural Engineering at the University of Wyoming. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Building Engineering and Construction Management from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology. His academic background is complemented by strong skills in building materials, construction management, AutoCAD, project management, and quality assurance.

At the University of Wyoming, Estiak is pursuing advanced coursework and research focused on the innovative applications of drone technology in architecture and construction. His work aims to improve building performance, construction efficiency, and sustainability through the integration of unmanned aerial systems.

Besides his academic roles, Estiak has diverse professional experience including serving as a Graduate Teaching Assistant, Transaction Service Officer at IFIC Bank PLC, and Vice President of BUILTECH FEST where he led design teams and website development. He is also experienced in operations and document management, course planning, and mentorship from his leadership roles at KUET and BUILTECH FEST.

Estiak’s comprehensive education and practical experience position him at the forefront of leveraging new technologies to address complex challenges in modern architectural engineering.
